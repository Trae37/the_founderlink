# 2025 Freelance Platform Developer Rates Research

## Summary of Findings

### Upwork
**Sources**: Official Upwork cost pages, Reddit discussions, industry blogs
- **Software Developers**: $10-$100/hr (median $20/hr)
- **Web Developers**: $15-$50/hr (median $30/hr)
- **Reality**: Most decent developers charge $30-$60/hr; experienced developers $60-$100/hr
- **Current code**: $30-$100/hr ✓ **ACCURATE**

### Toptal
**Sources**: Toptal pricing analysis, client reviews, industry comparisons
- **General Range**: $60-$150/hr for most roles
- **Specialized/AI**: Can reach $200+/hr
- **Reality**: Premium platform with higher quality, rates start at $60/hr minimum
- **Current code**: $60-$150/hr ✓ **ACCURATE**

### Fiverr
**Sources**: Fiverr official cost guides, project pricing data
- **Hourly**: $20-$200/hr (web design), $20-$250/hr (WordPress), $20-$400/hr (software dev)
- **Project-based**: $1,500-$100,000+ (typical $500-$10K for MVPs)
- **Reality**: Budget to mid-tier, highly variable quality
- **Current code**: "$500-$10K project" and "$10-$60/hr" ✓ **MOSTLY ACCURATE** (upper bound could be $200/hr)

### Gun.io
**Sources**: Gun.io official guides, pricing reviews
- **Freelance rates**: $60-$400/hr (US/Canada developers)
- **Typical range**: $100-$200/hr plus 20% fee for full-time hires
- **Reality**: Premium vetted platform, higher quality
- **Current code**: $80-$150/hr ⚠️ **NEEDS UPDATE** (should be $100-$200/hr)

### Contra
**Sources**: Contra pricing guides, freelance rate articles
- **Web developers**: $25-$200+/hr
- **Reality**: Commission-free platform, mid-tier pricing
- **Current code**: $50-$120/hr ⚠️ **NEEDS UPDATE** (should be $25-$150/hr to reflect full range)

### Freelancer.com
**Sources**: Industry comparisons, offshore rate data
- **General range**: $15-$80/hr (budget platform with global talent)
- **Reality**: Budget option with offshore developers, wide quality variance
- **Current code**: $15-$80/hr ✓ **ACCURATE**

## Recommended Updates

1. **Gun.io**: Change from $80-$150/hr to **$100-$200/hr**
2. **Contra**: Change from $50-$120/hr to **$40-$150/hr** (to better reflect entry to senior range)
3. **Fiverr**: Keep "$10-$60/hr" but note this is conservative (could go up to $200/hr)
